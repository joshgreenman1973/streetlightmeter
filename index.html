Your Reports

📱
Step 1 of 4
Go outside at night
Walk to a street or sidewalk where you think the lighting is poor. StreetLightMeter works best after dark — that's when lighting problems are visible.
🔦
Step 2 of 4
Hold your phone at chest height
Point the rear camera (the one on the back of your phone) outward, facing the street — not at the sky, not at the ground. Hold it roughly at chest height, like you're taking a photo of the block ahead of you.
Works with any phone — iPhone or Android
📷
Step 3 of 4
Tap the button and hold steady
Tap "Measure Light Here," then allow camera access if prompted. You'll see a live view from your camera. Tap the capture button and hold still for two seconds while the app analyzes the light.
📋
Step 4 of 4
Report it to 311
The app will show your reading and tell you if the area is dark, dim, or adequate. Pick the problem type, then file a report online, call 311, or text 311. Your reading is saved on the map so others can see it too.
